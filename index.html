About Jen
Real HR experience. Real AI tools.
Jennalyn Gamboa

Hi, I'm Jen — an HR professional and found that HR was never just about documents. It was always about people. Today, I help HR professionals work smarter using AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Canva AI and other HR AI tools — practical, beginner-friendly, and grounded in real HR experience.

My interest in HR started back in 2018 when I was still in college. At the time, I honestly thought HR was mostly paperwork — contracts, filing, and administrative tasks. But the longer I stayed in the field, the more I realized HR was never just about documents. It was about people.

Behind every policy is a person trying to build a better workplace. Behind every process is a team working toward growth. And behind every business goal are people doing their best to succeed together.

That realization changed the way I viewed this profession. Over the years, I've worked across hospitality, leisure, technology, and HR consultancy — supporting Organizational Development, Employee Experience, Performance Management, and HRBP functions for teams across the Philippines and globally.

CHRA CHRP® CMHFA LinkedIn Learning Claude AI Certified

Then 2023–2024 came — and like many professionals, I saw how quickly the world of work was changing. What started as simple curiosity with ChatGPT slowly became part of everyday workflow. Eventually, I began exploring Claude, Gemini, Canva AI and other AI tools — not to replace the human side of HR, but to support it.

The more repetitive work we can simplify, the more time we create for what truly matters — listening to employees, solving problems, building culture, and supporting better decisions.

I'm not here as an “AI expert.” I'm here as an HR professional who continues to learn, adapt, and explore practical ways to work smarter — while keeping the human side of HR at the center.

So if you're curious about HR, AI, People & Culture, or simply looking for practical ways to grow — welcome. You're in the right place.
